Malaysia became the forty-ninth country in my collection. A pencil from this country combines several features:

- It has a [shaved side](?tag=sliced), which is common
- It has a flower, exactly the same as on [Burgos](?display=burgos) or the [Thai five](?display=thaifive)
- It has a [doll](?display=unknowndoll), like on the Chinese pencil
