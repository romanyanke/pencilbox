Assisi is a town in the Italian region of Umbria, located in the province of Perugia, on the southern slope of Mount Subasio, situated high above the Tiber tributary Chiaso.

Assisi is the birthplace and place of religious activity of St. Francis of Assisi and St. Clare of Assisi. The patron saint of the city is St. Rufinus, the first bishop of Assisi. The city's day is celebrated on 12 August.
