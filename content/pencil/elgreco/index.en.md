A red pencil of standard length, but slightly thicker than usual. Thick lead made of red, white, and blue colors. When you write, the line goes red or blue plus all transitions between the three colors (white makes it paler, and at the junction of red and blue you get pink-purple).

It turns out that the [Museum of Contemporary Art in Helsinki](?display=kiasma) sells exactly the same pencil, but with a different inscription.
