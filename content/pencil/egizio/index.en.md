A gold-colored pencil from the Egyptian Museum in Turin, Italy. On one side is the logo and name — Museo Egizio, and on the other side the pencil is decorated with Egyptian writing whose meaning I could not decipher. At the end there is a stone-like decoration. It was brought in the museum envelope; it is in the photo.
