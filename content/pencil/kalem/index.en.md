This is the 87th pencil in my collection, but the first with a [triangular](?tag=triangle) shape. I was given it at the Carpet Museum in Baku when I asked for a pencil in the souvenir shop. They did not have any, but they gave me a simple office one that they use for writing/work. I thought "siyah kurşun kalem" was Azerbaijani, but the translator says it is "black pencil" in Turkish.
