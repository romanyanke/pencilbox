I did not expect to find such a pencil in Vladikavkaz, it looks more like some kind of Thailand. Actually, I bought it at Beslan airport before departure. A large piece of wood with thick blue lead. On the side it says "Hello from Ossetia!", and on the shaved end there is a flag: a white-red-yellow tricolor. It looks like the [Krasnoyarsk pencil](?display=krasnoyarsk). Price 150 rubles.
