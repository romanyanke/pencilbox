I found one only in a single store, but they were exactly like the ones from [Thailand](?display=thaifive) or [Albania](?display=albania), just with a different inscription. I decided to look more, because I wanted variety. I found another pencil only after 3 weeks. Of course it is very similar to those, but for the first time there is a keychain!
