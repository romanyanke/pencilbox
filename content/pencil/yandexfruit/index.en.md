In the transparent package, which holds 6 pencils and an insert repeating the pencil prints, it also says "Do not eat", because these pencils look like fruit sticks! Strawberry, watermelon, orange, banana, cucumber, and blueberry - each pencil repeats the color of the fruit/vegetable/berry. The pencils are square, I had to create a [square tag](?tag=square) for them. The fruit print is only on one side; on the other, colorless side, the pencils are taped together into a single strip. This was a gift from a colleague at Yandex for my birthday.

By the way, this is already the second unusual set of pencils from Yandex; there was also a [mini set](?display=miniyandex).
