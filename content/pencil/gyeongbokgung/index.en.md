Gyeongbokgung is a palace complex located in northern Seoul. One pencil shows Korean riders, the other shows people in traditional costumes. Each says "Garedogam Uigwe" - a phrase that yields 8 results in Google. And it also says 영조정순후가례도감의게, which Translate renders as "After the Encyclopedias Yeongjo jeongsun of garye", from which only "yeongjo" is clear - Yeongjo, the 21st king of the Joseon state.
