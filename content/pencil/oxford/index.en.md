The Bodleian Library is the library of Oxford University, which disputes the Vatican's right to be called the oldest in Europe, and the British Library's title of the largest book collection in the UK. The pencil itself has a cool heavy book made of some metal. Usually my pencils are divided into those with a [toy on the end](?tag=with%20a%20toy) or with an [eraser](?tag=with%20rubber). And this one has both, because with it there was a separate large eraser in the shape of a book.
